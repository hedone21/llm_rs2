## Notes
- Short context Decode 40.23 ms/tok and Avg TBT 44.42 ms are in the expected
  42-48 ms/tok range; the reason short-context Decode is actually slightly
  lower than long-context Decode is that at seq_len < 128 the flash kernel
  touches a small KV tile, so softmax/reduction dominates less than matmul.
- Long context Decode 41.56 ms/tok is within 0.4 ms of the forward_gen
  prototype reference (41.2 ms/tok at seq ~720), confirming the plan.rs
  StandardFlash variant is dispatching correctly. A full legacy fallback
  would have shown ~53.8 ms/tok.
- Generation stopped at 70 of 128 tokens because the sampler hit EOS on
  the long-context run; this is a model behavior, not a perf regression.
- Avg TBT 48.10 ms is ~6.5 ms higher than Decode because it includes the
  prefill→decode transition and the first-token cold path, which is
  expected at ~656-token prompts.
- No thermal throttling or driver warnings observed during the runs.
- Built with libs/aarch64/libOpenCL.so copied from the main worktree
  (the flash-attn-decode worktree was missing the OpenCL stub initially).
